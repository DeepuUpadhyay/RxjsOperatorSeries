              When to Use Take?

              ‚úÖ Use Take When:
              • Implementing pagination (first N items)
              • One-time operations (welcome messages)
              • Limiting retry attempts
              • Performance optimization
              • Preventing infinite streams
              • Getting sample data for testing
              ‚ùå Avoid Take When:
              • You need all the data
              • Dynamic limiting is required
              • The count might change at runtime
              • Working with finite observables
              üí° Pro Tip:

              Take operators are perfect for performance optimization. Use take(1) for one-time values, takeUntil() for cleanup, and take(n) for pagination!
